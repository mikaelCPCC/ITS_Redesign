Information Technology Services (ITS) is dedicated to the effective and efficient delivery of technology services to the CPCC community.

The services listed below have been identified as CPCC core services that impact significant percentage of the campus community and the day to day operations of the college.

The CPCC OSA System is provided as a snapshot look at the current state of core CPCC services. Due to the broad nature of this system it is still the responsibility of the individual to check the status of a service. Reliance on this system does not provide justification to halt any work.

For assistance using any of these services or if you suspect an interruption of service, contact the ITS Help Desk at helpdesk@cpcc.edu or call 704.330.5000.
